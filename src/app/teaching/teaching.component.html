Teaching

I am passionate about helping others learn, be inspired, and develop robust solutions to important problems. My primary goal as a teacher is to facilitate student development into life-long creators. Creating implies the purposeful application of learning. As life-long creators, students will exercise their abilities to meaningfully shape and control the world around them. Overall, I strive to build a learning atmosphere that fosters the development of life-long creators. This demands a student-driven classroom setting with partial student control of content and assessment determined by a dialogue between all participants in the course. In this manner, I attempt to provide the greatest value to students and the greater computing community. My full pdf teaching statement is available here
